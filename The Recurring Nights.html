The Recurring Nights

Concept Arts:

The Recurring Nights

Genre: 3D Horror, Survival, Psychological Thriller

Target Audience: Psychological Horror Enthusiasts, Casual and Hardcore Gamers and Horror Fans

Time to Complete Design: 1 day taken

Core Concept:

The Recurring Nights is a psychological horror game that immerses players in a chilling mystery set within the walls of a seemingly abandoned hospital. The protagonist, recovering from a car accident, finds themselves trapped in a hospital that is far from ordinary. As they struggle to regain their bearings, strange and nightmarish events begin to unfold, pushing the protagonist to question what is real and what is part of their own unraveling nightmare.

As the story progresses, the protagonist navigates the eerie hospital floors, piecing together clues that point to the building’s dark past—and a deeper connection to the protagonist’s own troubled history. The player must solve puzzles, interact with mysterious objects, and uncover hidden secrets while battling paranormal threats like shadowy figures, whispers, and haunting hallucinations.

Each failed attempt to break free from the nightmare only reinforces the protagonist’s doubts about their sanity and the nature of their experiences. In The Recurring Nights, the player must navigate the fine line between reality and delusion, as they uncover the terrifying truth behind their circumstances and the supernatural forces at play.


Key Features:

1. Exploration & Interaction: The player searches the hospital, interacting with objects and collecting clues that are necessary to progress.

2. Mental Health Management: The protagonist’s fear and anxiety levels must be monitored to avoid mental breakdowns, which can distort perception and hinder progress.

3. Puzzle-Solving: The player will need to solve environmental puzzles related to the hospital’s dark history and the protagonist’s trauma to uncover new clues and unlock the next steps.

4. Survival Mechanics: As the player explores, they face random death traps and confront shadowy figures and paranormal threats.

5. Cyclical Gameplay: Each night is a new attempt to escape. If the player succeeds, they break free; if they fail, they wake up back in their hospital bed, beginning the loop again.
