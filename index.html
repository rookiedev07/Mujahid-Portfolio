Project Gallery

Nightmare: Dusk of Regret

  • Genre: 2D Side-Scrolling, Story-Based Survival with Parkour Gameplay
  • Platform: PC/Mobile
  • Game Concept: In this survival game, players take on the role of Dr. Maxwell Reeves, a scientist responsible for the outbreak of a zombie virus. As the world crumbles, Dr. Reeves must navigate a ruined city using parkour to avoid both zombies and environmental hazards. Along the way, he seeks redemption by working to create an antidote that could save the remaining survivors.
  • Key Features:
  • Parkour Movement: Maxwell navigates a decaying city using parkour, improving his skills to dodge zombies and access hard-to-reach areas, with his emotional struggles impacting survival.
  • Combat with Zombies: Combat is tense, with limited resources. Maxwell uses improvised weapons and bare hands for survival, with quick-time events and timed attacks adding intensity. Avoiding combat is essential to conserve resources.
  • Emotional Narrative and Flashbacks: Uncover Maxwell's emotional journey through flashbacks and cutscenes, with player choices shaping the narrative and the world’s response.

Lost Signal: Outpost - X

  • Genre: 3D Story Driven Puzzle-Combat Survival
  • Platform: PC
  • Game Concept: In this gripping survival game, Dr. Ethan Ward, a scientist recently returned from a black hole mission, discovers the creation of a dangerous entity that threatens humanity’s survival. As Ethan, players must navigate through puzzles, engage in intense combat, and face moral decisions that shape the course of the story and determine humanity’s fate.
  • Combat Mechanics: Real-Time Combat: Fast-paced, fluid combat where Ethan’s movements and attacks require reflexes and strategy.
  • Quick-Time Events: Players press specific buttons during key moments to overcome threats or gain advantages.
  • Freerange Exploration: Expansive environments with hidden secrets, collectibles, and resources.
  • Zero-Gravity Movement: In low-gravity areas, players use jetpacks or anti-gravity tools to navigate obstacles and combat.
  • Cutscene Integration: High-quality cinematic cutscenes advance the story, offering emotional depth and insights into Ethan’s journey, Outpost-X, and the entity threat.

The Reccurring Nights

  • Genre: 3D Horror, Survival, Psychological Thriller
  • Platform: PC & Mobile
  • Game Concept: The Recurring Nights is a psychological horror game where the protagonist, trapped in an eerie hospital after a car accident, must solve puzzles and survive paranormal threats. As they try to escape, they uncover dark secrets about the hospital and their own past, blurring reality with nightmare.
  • Exploration & Interaction: The player searches the hospital, interacting with objects and collecting clues necessary to progress.
  • Mental Health Management: Monitor the protagonist’s fear and anxiety levels to avoid breakdowns that distort perception and hinder progress.
  • Puzzle-Solving: Solve environmental puzzles tied to the hospital’s dark history and the protagonist’s trauma to uncover clues and unlock new steps.
  • Survival Mechanics: Face random death traps, shadowy figures, and paranormal threats while exploring.
  • Cyclical Gameplay: Each night is a new attempt to escape; succeed to break free, or fail and wake up in the hospital bed, restarting the loop.
